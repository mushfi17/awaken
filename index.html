~ About Us ~

DR KATE ZOUEV

FOUNDER AND DIRECTOR OF AWAKEN ACUPUNCTURE

Kate’s passion for natural medicine began in 2009 when her mother was diagnosed with an
aggressive, terminal cancer. Kate was introduced to Acupuncture and Naturopathy as part of
her mothers’ palitive care and it was extraordinarily helpful at increasing her quality of life and
time left on earth.

DR LAUREN MONAGHAN

ACUPUNCTURIST

Lauren has an extensive background in both natural and western medicine. She started her career
as a pathology specimen collector after studying laboratory technology, before taking a interest in
natural medicine. Lauren has also worked as a clinical assistant at various acupuncture clinics since 2014.

SHEILA MURRAY

COUNSELLOR - BHSC., MCOUNS & PSCYHTH.

Sheila Murray holds a Master’s degree in Counselling and Psychotherapy. Originally from Ireland,
she has had a 30-year career in holistic health, Naturopathy and wellbeing. Sheila’s lived experience
and wisdom are what she brings to her practice. Her current work in counselling and psychotherapy
focuses on supporting those undergoing the journey into parenthood, whether naturally, via IVF, IUI,
adoption or any other means, the creation of a family begins from the moment the idea is conceived.
